How to submit your ITRF?
Return Forms can be submitted by two (2) methods:

Manually to Tax Information & Record Management Section, LHDNM. Kindly refer guide notes / explanatory notes which can be accessed from IRBM official portal before filling up. IRBM official portal, www.hasil.gov.my > Forms > Download Forms > Individual > Year of Assessment.

Online via MyTax system at https://mytax.hasil.gov.my
